# Code Of Conduct

TalkType is a small project, but people still matter.

Use respectful language. Keep criticism specific, useful, and about the work. Harassment, hate, slurs, ableism, racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, personal attacks, or bad-faith pile-ons are not welcome here.

The maintainer may edit, hide, lock, or delete issues and comments, and may block people who make the project harder or less safe for others.

In short: be direct, be kind, and help keep the place useful.
